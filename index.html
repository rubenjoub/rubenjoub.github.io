About Me

I'm a dedicated and versatile designer and developer with a passion for creating smart, functional, and engaging solutions. My skill set spans front-end web development, 3D CAD modeling, and embedded systems using microcontrollers such as the ESP32.

I have hands-on experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and GSAP for building responsive, interactive websites. I also work with CAD tools like SolidWorks to design and prototype mechanical components.

In addition, I’ve built projects using the ESP32 and other microcontrollers — from sensor-based systems to connected IoT devices — where I combine electronics with programming to solve real-world problems. This blend of hardware and software expertise allows me to approach problems holistically, from concept through to functional prototype.

I'm currently looking for opportunities to grow professionally, contribute to innovative projects, and work with teams that value curiosity, technical skill, and creativity.
